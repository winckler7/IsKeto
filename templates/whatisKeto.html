Ketogenic Diet

What is?

The ketogenic diet, often referred to as the keto diet, is a popular and low-carbohydrate diet that has gained recognition for its potential health benefits. This diet is designed to help individuals achieve a state of ketosis, where the body primarily burns fat for energy instead of carbohydrates bringing the body back to it's default energy source.

Key Principles

  • Low Carbohydrate Intake: The core principle of the keto diet is to significantly reduce carbohydrate consumption.
  • Moderate Protein: Protein intake is moderate on the keto diet.
  • High Healthy Fat: The majority of calories come from healthy fats.

How It Works

When you consume fewer carbohydrates, your body's glucose levels decrease, and it starts to burn stored fat for energy. This process leads to the production of molecules called ketones, which are used for fuel. Achieving and maintaining ketosis is the goal of the ketogenic diet.

Benefits

The ketogenic diet offers several potential benefits:

  • Weight Loss
  • Stable Blood Sugar
  • Improved Mental Clarity
  • Improved levels of energy
  • Improved skin health

Considerations

  • Consult a Healthcare Professional
  • Possible Side Effects
  • Not Suitable for Everyone
